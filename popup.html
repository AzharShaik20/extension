How to use AI Prompter

Getting Started

Simply type your rough idea or concept in the text area above. The AI will transform it into a detailed, creative prompt.

Examples

  • "I want a website like Amazon"
  • "Create a mobile app for fitness tracking"
  • "Design a logo for my coffee shop"
  • "Write a story about time travel"

Tips

  • Be specific about your goals
  • Include target audience if relevant
  • Mention any style preferences
  • Keep it under 1000 characters

Troubleshooting

If you're getting errors, make sure:

  • Your backend server is running on localhost:5000
  • You have a valid Gemini API key configured
  • Your internet connection is stable
