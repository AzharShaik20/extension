Privacy Policy for AI Prompter Chrome Extension

Last updated: January 8, 2025

1. Data Collection

AI Prompter collects minimal data to provide its core functionality:

  • User Input Text: The prompts you type are sent to our backend server for processing
  • Generated Responses: AI-generated prompts are temporarily stored locally in your browser
  • Usage Analytics: Basic usage statistics (no personal information)

2. Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing your input through Google's Gemini AI API
  • Returning refined prompts to you
  • Storing recent prompts locally for your convenience

3. Data Storage

Local Storage: Recent prompts are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Server Processing: Your input is temporarily processed by our backend server and forwarded to Google's Gemini AI API. No data is permanently stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

AI Prompter uses Google's Gemini AI API to generate prompts. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information about how Google handles your data.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • API key protection (never exposed to client-side code)
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Input validation and sanitization

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Google's Gemini AI API (for prompt generation)
  • When required by law or legal process

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local storage data using the extension's "Clear" button
  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Children's Privacy

AI Prompter is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

This privacy policy complies with Chrome Web Store requirements and applicable privacy laws.
